Welcome to Atomic Modules

The Atomic Modules is develped by a group of students who find it hard to select the right modules.

What is Atomic Modules?

Atomic Modules is a platform where you can find feedbacks and ratings about all the modules that students have taken in the past.

What can you contribute to the platform?

In Atomic Modules, you can rate and provide feedbacks about the modules you have taken. This info will then be shared with other students.
